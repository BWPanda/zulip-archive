indigoxela (Aug 26 2020 at 11:08):

(Curiosity is my cryptonite...)
in YOURTHEME_preprocess_layout()

  if ($node = menu_get_object()) {
    $variables['title'] = filter_xss($node->title, array('strong', 'span', 'em'));
  }

In YOURTHEME_preprocess_node:

  $node = $variables['node'];
  $variables['title'] = filter_xss($node->title, array('strong', 'span', 'em'));

That keeps strong/span/em and strips anything else.
