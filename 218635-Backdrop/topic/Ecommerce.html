Phil Ward (Mar 01 2021 at 20:17):

They aren't crazy changes @BWPanda so you don't have to worry about hard criticism :wink:. Just tricks of the trade I've learned over the years.

1) move the add-to-cart right below the price. Conversions are higher when the add-to-cart is up high and within or very close to above the fold.

2) this will be a bit more difficult, but see if you can figure out a way to move the product title above the images for mobile.

Its fine where it is on desktop (to the right and above the price) and it would be fine on mobile if there were 4-5 thumbnail images but the 15-20 images on mobile shove the title below the images so it creates a "what am I looking at?" moment.
