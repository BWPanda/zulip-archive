BWPanda (Nov 01 2020 at 23:15):

Ok, so Featherlight works:

<a href="#" data-featherlight="node/add/page #page-node-form">Add page</a>

The #page-node-form part after the URL is a selector that tells Featherlight which part of the page to display; in this case just the form (rather than the whole page (header, menu, footer, etc.)). While that looks much better, it unfortunately has the apparent effect of not loading the JS needed for certain form items (such as the vertical tabs). Looking into a fix for that now...
