mazze.ch (Sep 13 2020 at 13:05):

Regarding commerce... I really like Ubercart, clicked through a couple of weeks ago. I did several bigger D7 Commerce sites in the past, and still maintain some of them. Ubercart is great for simple shops, the setup is quite easy. However, porting Commerce makes no sense IMO, it requires a lot of other modules to be ported, and the concept feels more and more outdated. I am not even sure if I should migrate the other shops to D9, it seems that Commerce 2.x can't keep up with the pace of the market. Important things still do not work properly.
