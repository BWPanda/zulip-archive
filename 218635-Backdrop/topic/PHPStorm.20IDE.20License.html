jenlampton (Jan 10 2021 at 21:34):

klonos said:

jenlampton or quicksketch perhaps add it to our 1password account, so that people with access can provide the license to contributors when requested?

The PHPStorm licenses are granted to specific people by JetBrains. There is not a single token that anyone can use for a license, instead we send a registraton-link to individual email addresses and those people create jetbrains accounts, and the license is granted to the account. @klonos I think there is a jetbrains log-in in 1Password, but if not I'll add it there today. The PHPStorm licenses expire every year in December and we need to re-apply for permission to use them for our OSS project. It's a bit of a hassle, and poor @Graham Oliver always gets stuck pestering me to re-apply for Backdrop. (Sorry about the delay this year @Graham Oliver )
