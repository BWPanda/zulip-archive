mazze.ch (Mar 16 2020 at 07:47):

A lot has been said about the the virus... Here in Switzerland, the infection rate has tripled(!)within the past 24 hours. A complete shutdown for malls, stores, restaurants is expected to be announced today.

But let me tell a tiny story about Backdrop CMS.

Saturday, I received a call from a restaurant customer. He quickly changed the concept for the weekend towards a takeaway service. I took my laptop, and we quickly setup a landing page, a newsletter and a social media post to inform people that the restaurant is open Sunday (= yesterday). The response was overwhelming, a lot of people sent congrats to the restaurant owner, for his good idea (the venue is pretty popular over the weekends, great view to the Alps).

And guess what? Backdrop really made a difference for me. Sure, Drupal 8 or any other CMS can also setup a text-only page within a few minutes. But I was able to change also the layout and a few other details very quickly, right at the restaurant table. The customer was pretty impressed (the old CMS was Contao, quite popular here, and quite complicated).

Today, I will continue to build my SMB platform on Backdrop, where config management plays an important role. There will be more customers which need a helping hand in the weeks to come, and Backdrop’s *flexibility” will be the base for the solution.

So @StPaulTim @BWPanda (Peter) @klonos, @docwilmot, @jenlampton @Nate Lampton and everyone that I forgot to mention... I just wanted so say that your work, your attention to details really counts, also during these times. All the best to you and your beloved ones.
