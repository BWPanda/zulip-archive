I was going to make a new minor release of Entity Plus after the latest PR was merged (thanks @earlyburg!) and was curious: we are at 1.0.9 right now. Can I do 1.0.10 or should I do 1.1.0? (There is already a 1.0.1 if that makes a difference).

indigoxela (Nov 12 2020 at 13:09):

It might depend on how "big" the changelog/release is. If it's just a minor thing, my feeling is that 1.0.10 is the best choice. Feedback by someone with better understanding of the Backdrop packaging system might be helpful, though.

klonos (Nov 12 2020 at 13:56):

I don't think that there is any problem with versions like 1.0.999999... or anything that would cause the packaging script to fail because of that. I agree with @indigoxela re if small changes and tiny bug fixes, then go with the bug number increment; if more substantial changes, then minor number; if major refactoring or backwards incompatible changes, then up the major number. In general https://semver.org should help :smile:
