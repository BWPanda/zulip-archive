klonos (Jun 04 2020 at 09:07):

Hello @Søren Bonde :wave: ...I believe that it is simply a matter of order (time-wise) of when the release tags have happened. Although https://github.com/backdrop/backdrop/tags seems to be sorted by tag number, if you hover your mouse over the time of the tag creation, you will see that 1.15.3 was created on May 20, at 22:21 EEST, whereas 1.16.1 has been created at 22:18. ...I guess we need to make this part of our release process, and make sure that we cut the tag for the previous minor version before the next minor. ( cc @Nate Lampton )
