indigoxela (May 05 2020 at 07:38):

@Jeff Porten definitely not springtime Christmas, but you can usually access all variables in preprocessor functions. https://api.backdropcms.org/api/backdrop/core%21modules%21system%21theme.api.php/function/hook_preprocess_HOOK/1

These hooks go to your theme's template.php

Try: YOURTHEME_preprocess_page(&$variables) or YOURTHEME_preprocess_node(&$variables)

To figure out what's in those "$variables", try debug($variables); in the preprocessor of your choice. Hope this helps, although coding is required.
