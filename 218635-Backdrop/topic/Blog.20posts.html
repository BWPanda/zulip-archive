jenlampton (Dec 29 2020 at 07:17):

I'm open to a new vocab, but we don't have 'themers' or 'site builders' in
Backdrop.

We have backend developers (PHP/MySQL) frontend developers (HTML/CSS/JS)
and site architects (no end / all ends).

The intent with our terminology is to give everyone in our community a
little more respect than they got in the Drupal community.

Front end developers are developers too, let's not give them a title that
indicates that they aren't -- especially a title that isn't even a real
word (and since there is a difference between backed devs and frontend
devs, both types need the modifier).

I find it interesting that you instinctively group 'backend' with the site
architects, and not with the PHP/MySQL coders. Have you seen that
definition someplace else?
