BWPanda (Dec 28 2020 at 01:37):

One thing that always seems to trip me up when theming a site is when I want to position something absolutely. The Contextual Links module adds position: relative; in lots of places, so I'm able to position sub-items absolutely without any fuss. But then I hand a site over to the client and everything looks weird. Because they don't have the Contextual Links showing for their account (also Anonymous users), then there's no position: relative;. Obviously I just need to remember to specifically add position: relative; where I need it, but it's so hard to remember to do that when things just work as an admin with the Contextual Links module in use... There's probably no easy solution for this other than trying to remember this, but just wondering if others struggle with the same problem...?
