BWPanda (Oct 28 2020 at 01:16):

As for your question, it looks like your bullet list is currently in the Body field of your page. If this is your only list of partners (i.e. you don't plan on having a different list on each page), my recommendation would be to setup a new content type (called 'Partner' for example) that has fields for the logo and link/URL. Then create new Partner content for each of your bullet points (and then remove them from the Body field). Once you have your Partners as separate content in a new content type, you can use the power of Views to output them in the method you desire (see @StPaulTim's comment above).
