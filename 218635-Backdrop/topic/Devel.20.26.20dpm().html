indigoxela (Sep 02 2020 at 11:35):

I have no tricks for dpm(), but you could use debug() in code directly:

Huge objects (nodes/views):
debug(array_keys(get_object_vars($node)));

Huge forms:
debug(element_children($form));

Huge arrays:
debug(array_keys($whatever));
