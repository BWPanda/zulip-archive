StPaulTim (Oct 01 2020 at 06:41):

My key thoughts about tomorrow (my opinions):

1) Don't overwhelm folks with too much information all at once. Just get them what they need to get started and then keep providing additional help throughout the day.
2) In Drupal sprints, we've learned the importance of getting people to post a comment in the issue queue. This is a big step and allows others in the community to offer advice and help.
3) At in-person beginner sprints, I spend a lot of time going around and asking people how they are doing and if they need help. We need to figure out the best way to do that in this virtual environment. We may need to keep a list and be sure that we reach out to every active participant a couple of times during the day.
