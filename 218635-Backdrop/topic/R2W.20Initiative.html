klonos (Dec 10 2020 at 14:35):

@docwilmot the hope is that by having that demo site available:

  • contrib theme authors will test their themes there and makes sure they work as expected
  • members of the community and existing users of themes will be able to confirm issues (that are not specific to their site) and report them to the theme issue queues (by pointing to a demo page of the r2w demo site with the issue)
  • potential Backdrop adopters can use that site to see what their site will look like
  • we could link to the r2w demo site from theme nodes in b.org, so that instead of screenshots (or in addition to them), people will be able to see a "live" version of the theme, along with some dummy content
    Don't you think that that's useful?
