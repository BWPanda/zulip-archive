@BWPanda may I ask why the namespace change? IIRC some people mentioned that one of the advantages of the project b over drush and brush was that they had to type less ...not that this cannot be achieved via an alias command - just less work to do if it's called just b.

BWPanda (Mar 16 2021 at 02:19):

There was a disconnect between the official name of the project "Backdrop Console" and the documented command b. Since people would naturally just refer to the project as b, then that became its 'unofficial' name, and some people felt that it would be better as something slightly longer and that had meaning outside of just a letter of the alphabet (to make searches for/references to it easier too). I was initially against the idea, as I liked the simplicity of b, but I eventually came around to the idea, especially after I thought of Bee (not too long, more unique, doesn't change how people pronounce it) :smile:

You can read more here: https://github.com/backdrop-contrib/bee/issues/132

Change the documented command name from `b`? · Issue #132 · backdrop-contrib/bee
I have two suggested project names: bcli bbash I like bcli, because it's short and means something - backdrop command line interface! bbash is also nice, with some cadence with the double '...

BWPanda (Mar 16 2021 at 02:22):

And yes, if people don't like typing too much, they can just change the installation steps slightly to use a different alias/symlink. I personally have a bunch of bash aliases on my computer for Drush commands like:

  • dca = drush cc all
  • ddl = drush dl
  • etc.
    So I imagine I'll do a similar thing on my computer with Bee once I start using it more regularly for day-to-day stuff.
