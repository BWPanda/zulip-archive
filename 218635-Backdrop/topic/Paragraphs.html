Phil Ward (Mar 10 2021 at 03:51):

Title + Body to create structured sub containers of content

Title + Table to create a structured table field

Title + Body + Image to create a structured container with info and an image

I'll use specific paragraphs on content types where some of the pages might need the paragraph and others don't. For example product variations. (The overall concept is simple, variations themselves are indepth)

The great thing about paragraphs is being able to structure information without requiring every page to have the exact same structure of information.

Side thought: it would be cool to merge bricks and paragraphs together somehow... Paragraphs for the backend and bricks for the UI or something.
