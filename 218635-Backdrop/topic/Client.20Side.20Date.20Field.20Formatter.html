klonos (Mar 15 2021 at 00:03):

@Phil Ward I do not fully understand what you posted above, but I'm familiar with these 2 issues re timezones:

  • There was debate over the auto-detection of the timezone during installation: should the installer default the timezone to whatever is configured on the server where the site is being installed, or the timezone of the browser of the person performing the installation? (think site hosted in the US, but installation and configuration is happening remotely, by a person in Australia)
  • After we had enough people register in the main b.org site, we thought that it'd be a good idea to ask them to add their country/timezone to their user profiles. Most/many people configure that, but because the field does not allow setting "none", if someone skipped setting their timezone, then it would default to Afghanistan or something like that (because alphabetical) ...same thing for user profiles created before we added the timezone/country field. ...we only found that out when we tried to get a count of people registered in b.org by country/timezone.

...just some things to be aware or think about.
