Phil Ward (Nov 13 2020 at 09:07):

So, I chat with my sister today who works for Google to see what her thoughts were on the best approach for collecting errors and phoning them home, as brought up in the weekly meeting.

Her suggestion was to collect the top generated errors and send them off once per day, or maybe once per week and then aggregate them but she agreed that it would give the devs insight into problems they aren't able to see right now.

I think it would require some tweaking to get a nice balance between data and not overwhelming the receiving server, but my intention was to provide almost real-time data on release stability initially for core and later contribs.

Maybe this idea is a moot point due to the automated testing I understand to be coming, however, I know the tests can only go so far and real-world data is the real benchmark in my mind.
