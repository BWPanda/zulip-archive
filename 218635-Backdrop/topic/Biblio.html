klonos (Jun 23 2020 at 02:43):

The advantage of paragraphs over multifield is that you can mix and match paragraph types, and that content editors can reorder them. With multifields you don't get that much flexibility, as what types of subfields are included in each multifield is specific with each "instance" or "item".

jenlampton (Jun 23 2020 at 18:49):

@Herb I prefer multifield because it does not make your site slow :) All the field data is stored in the field table for the multifield, not other field tables that need to be joined to, so it makes queries much faster, especially when you have lots of data in them.
