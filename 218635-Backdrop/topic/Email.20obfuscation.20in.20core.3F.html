BWPanda (Jan 21 2021 at 06:59):

In case anyone's wondering,... Email obfuscation is an anti-spam technique where email addresses are converted into code that the browser can read (so users still see the regular email address), but spambots can't. To varying degrees of success. This is what the Invisimail contrib module does, so this issue is basically about getting that into core.

Phil Ward (Jan 21 2021 at 20:01):

I feel like this is important to protect people who are new to the web and don't understand the ramifications of putting an email address out on a website.
