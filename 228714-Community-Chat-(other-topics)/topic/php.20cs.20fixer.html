Geoff St. Pierre (serundeputy) (Jul 04 2020 at 13:19):

@Robert Garrigos

lando composer require backdrop/coder

in your .lando.yml file:

tooling:
  phpcs:
    service: appserver
    cmd:
      - "/app/vendor/bin/phpcs -n --report=full --standard=Backdrop --ignore=vendor/*,README.md --extensions=install,module,php,inc,theme"

Robert Garrigos (Jul 04 2020 at 14:45):

thanks @Geoff St. Pierre (serundeputy) I was doing it right, but just slightly different. I see what was the problem. First, I also installed drupal/coder, which installed a different version of php_codesniffer. Installing only backdrop/coder fixed that. But also, I needed to set Backdrop as standard with 

lando phpcs --config-set installed_paths /app/vendor/backdrop/coder/coder_sniffer

otherwise I got:

ERROR: the "Backdrop" coding standard is not installed. The installed coding standards are MySource, PEAR, PHPCS, PSR1, PSR2, Squiz and Zend

Now it works. However, I got this:
