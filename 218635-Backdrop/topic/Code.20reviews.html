@docwilmot to answer your question: no, you cannot hover on touch devices, unless you are using a mouse (think laptops like MS Surface and the likes, which are still large-screen devices, support touch/pen, but people also connect mice to them). Hover with touch can only be "simulated" ...to elaborate on that, the current "Use a click to open, instead of a hover" option will have the following result, depending on device:

  • devices with mice/pointer support: mouse hover will not expand the menu; you need to click once to expand, and then a second time to visit the link
  • touch-only devices w/o pointer: hover is not possible; you need to tap once to expand, and then a second time to visit the link
