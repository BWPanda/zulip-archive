docwilmot (Oct 10 2020 at 21:41):

The ‘browse server’ I was thinking of was strictly for files.

In that case IMCE should be enough for this then. You can make a tiny custom module with only the following code in the module file:

/**
 * Implements hook_form_FORM_ID-alter().
 *
 * Adds a "Browse server" link to the backdroplink dialog.
 *
 * @see filter_format_editor_image_form().
 */

function MODULE_form_filter_format_editor_link_form_alter(&$form, &$form_state) {
  $form['href']['#id'] = 'urlField';
  $openFileBrowser =
    '$("#ckeditor-modal .form-type-managed-file").hide();'
  . '$("#ckeditor-modal .form-item-attributes-src").show();'
  . 'window.open("' . base_path()
  . 'imce?app=backdrop|url@urlField", "", "width=760,height=560,resizable=1");';
  $form['href']['#suffix'] = "<input type=button onclick='javascript:"
    . $openFileBrowser . "' value='Browse server' style='float:right;'>";
}
