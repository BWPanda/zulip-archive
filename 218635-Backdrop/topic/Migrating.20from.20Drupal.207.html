indigoxela (Jun 02 2020 at 12:40):

Did your Drupal site have a field_image anyway? I guess, you followed this instruction: https://backdropcms.org/upgrade-from-drupal

If your Drupal didn't have a field named field_image and the database table doesn't exist now, but the config exists, ... I suspect it's the default field that ships with Backdrop on install (attached to node type "post").

If you do not need it anyway for your upgraded Drupal, you better delete it in Backdrop before you switch the database to your Drupal db. Hope this helps, I'm guessing a lot here...
