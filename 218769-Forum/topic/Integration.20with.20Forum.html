I happened to see while clicking around Zulip at lunch time that you can post to a stream via an email address. An easy way to get an initial forum integration with the backdrop forum and Zulip would be to get the forum site to send an email to that "Forum" stream's email address when a new topic is added. e.g. Email subject becomes the Zulip topic.
https://zulipchat.com/help/message-a-stream-by-email

Message a stream by email (Zulip Help Center)
You can send emails to Zulip streams. This can be useful | If you have an email that you want to discuss on Zulip | For mirroring mailing list traffic | For integrating a third-party app that can send emails, but which does not easily lend itself to a more direct integration | If you're planning on doing this in an automated way, and have some programming experience, another option is to use our send message API. | The email subject will become the Zulip topic, and the email body will become the Zulip message.
