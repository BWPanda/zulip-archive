Laryn (Apr 01 2020 at 17:28):

@indigoxela Basically you need:

  1. An exposed filter above the Dependent Filter and an exposed filter underneath it.
  2. Then you can configure the Dependent Filter to use the one that is above as a "Controller" and the one that is beneath as a "Dependent".
  3. When the value of the Controller field matches what you've specified, the Dependent field should show, otherwise it should be hidden.
