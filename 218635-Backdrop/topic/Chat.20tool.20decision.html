StPaulTim (Feb 04 2020 at 21:34):

I thought I would solicit some input from fresh eyes. Not sure if I'll get any, but it's worth a try.

https://twitter.com/StPaulTim/status/1224806640953765890

The @backdropcms community is looking for feedback on which chat tool would be easier to join AND use for our community. We want to avoid the temptation to cater to the power users in our group and remain as accessible AND usable to the larger community. (1 of 2)

- Tim Erickson (@StPaulTim)

StPaulTim (Feb 04 2020 at 21:36):

I believe that some people are concerned that moving from Gitter to Zulip will serve the needs of power users in our community and limit or discourage new people from joining. I disagree with that, but would love to get input from someone that is not already active in our chat community.

StPaulTim (Feb 04 2020 at 21:53):

@Herb - I don't disagree. But, I am also sympathetic with the desire to use the tools that are mostly likely to appeal to the broadest audience. As you know, I favor Zulip and tend to believe that we'll get more, not less, participation in Zulip, but I understand this concern.

I think that we can learn from the long standing tension in the Drupal community over the use of IRC or Slack. Power users loved IRC, but it was not at all welcoming to new users.

But it also allows "power users" to opt in to more complex streams that might deter novices at first glance (e.g. Code Review)
